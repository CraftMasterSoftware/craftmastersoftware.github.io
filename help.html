CraftMaster Help


Take control of your worlds

Streamlined management of saved worlds and more efficient in-game navigation

With CraftMaster it’s easy to manage your Minecraft worlds, saving time and minimizing headaches. And, in-game navigation is a breeze using the fully-contoured topographical 2D maps generated by CraftMaster. Easily find signs and structures, teleport with a single click of the mouse, and more.

 

Create like never before

Import 3D models and exchange schematics to produce amazing Minecraft creations

CraftMaster allows you to leverage the creativity and expertise of designers and architects from around the world by making it easy to import 3D models from sites like SketchUp’s 3D Warehouse. In a just few minutes you can produce iconic structures like the Eiffel Tower or the Sydney Opera House in your worlds. You can also easily import Minecraft schematics shared by your friends, or downloaded from professional sites like BlockWorks.

 

Exciting new ways to share

Share your Minecraft creations with family and friends on Sketchfab and social media

CraftMaster lets you easily share the amazing structures you create via Sketchfab, the world’s leading 3D community site. Once on Sketchfab, you can view your Minecraft creations using VR, and you can easily post your fully-navigable 3D models on Facebook, as well as other social media sites.
